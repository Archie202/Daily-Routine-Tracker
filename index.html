Daily Routine Tracker

6:00-6:15 AM: Wake Up and Hydrate

Wake Up: Aim to wake up at 6:00 AM consistently.

Hydrate: Start your day with a glass of water to rehydrate your body.

6:15-6:30 AM: Light Exercise or Stretching

Exercise: Do a short, light exercise routine or stretching to get your blood flowing. This can include yoga, a quick walk, or a few basic exercises.

6:30-7:00 AM: Mindfulness or Meditation

Mindfulness/Meditation: Spend 10-15 minutes meditating or practicing mindfulness. This can help you clear your mind and set a positive tone for the day.

7:00-7:30 AM: Healthy Breakfast

Breakfast: Have a nutritious breakfast. Include proteins, healthy fats, and whole grains to keep your energy levels steady.

7:30-8:00 AM: Plan Your Day

Planning: Spend this time organizing your day. Prioritize your tasks and set clear goals for your study session and work.

8:00-9:00 AM: Learning and Personal Development

Learning: Dedicate this hour to reading, learning something new, or personal development activities. This will prepare your mind for the study session.

9:00 AM - 12:00 PM: Focused Study Time

Study: Begin your study session. Break your study time into focused intervals (e.g., 50 minutes of study with a 10-minute break).

Tips to Avoid Distractions

Phone Management: Avoid checking your phone first thing in the morning. Instead, set specific times to check messages.

Task Prioritization: Use tools like to-do lists or apps to prioritize important tasks and minimize time spent on less important activities.

Evening Preparation

Night Before: Prepare for the next day the evening before. Set out your clothes, organize your workspace, and create a rough plan for your morning.
